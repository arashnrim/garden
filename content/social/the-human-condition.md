In the [[human-ai-interaction|interactions I've had with AI]] so far, I'm starting to realise a pattern in which I begin asking it more deeper questions about the human condition: anywhere from [[hedonic-adaptation|the hedonic treadmill]] and whether we should work against it to having a meta-conversation on why some people feel hooked to engage with [[large-language-models|LLMs]] like partners capable of emotional reasoning and reciprocation.

I realise that it's not that healthy to engage in this kind of behaviour — conversing, engaging with something non-human about traits that only humans themselves can experience and answer for. This collection are topics that I'd love to do a deep dive into, and one that doesn't involve talking to LLMs and anthropomorphising them to be an approximation of humans.
