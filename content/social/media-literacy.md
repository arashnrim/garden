Media literacy can refer to the ability to access, analyze, evaluate, create, and act using all forms of communication.

> Media literacy is the ability to encode and decode the symbols transmitted via media and synthesize, analyze and produce mediated messages.
> ...
> Media literacy represents a necessary, inevitable, and realistic response to the complex, ever-changing electronic environment and communication cornucopia surrounding us.
> 
> — [*The National Association for Media Literacy Education*](https://namle.org/resources/media-literacy-defined/)
